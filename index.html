About Us

We are a South African-based team dedicated to helping matriculants make informed decisions about their university applications. Our University Admission Tool allows learners to select their top three universities, using their matric subjects and results to provide a calculated probability of admission.

Our goal is to empower students with clear insights and improve their chances of gaining entry into higher education. All while helping you avoid unnecessary costs and efforts applying to universities that are unlikely to accept your applications.
